# Barneys New York

**Role:** Digital Manager
**Tenure:** August 2014 – June 2016
**Location:** New York, NY

## Overview

Early career role at one of New York's most iconic luxury retailers. Focused on improving the digital experience and bridging the gap between online and in-store operations at a time when omnichannel retail was still being figured out.

## What I Did

- Improved site architecture, checkout flows, and omnichannel systems to streamline the customer experience
- Partnered with engineering and retail operations to synchronize inventory, reducing errors and improving in-store pickup and fulfillment accuracy
- Collaborated with merchandising and buying teams to align digital product placement with inventory levels
- Improved sell-through rates and reduced overstock through better digital-to-inventory alignment

## Context

This was where I first learned how to work across functions — engineering, retail ops, merchandising — to ship things that actually worked in the real world. The challenge at Barneys was connecting a legacy retail operation to a modern digital experience, which meant a lot of alignment work and understanding systems that weren't designed to talk to each other.
