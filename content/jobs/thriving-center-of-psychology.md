# Thriving Center of Psychology

**Role:** Head of Product (promoted from Marketing & Founding Product Lead)
**Tenure:** December 2020 – April 2025
**Location:** New York, NY

## Overview

Joined TCP as the first product hire when the company was a small multi-state mental health platform. Left as Head of Product after driving the company from early-stage to $20M ARR with the same headcount. Owned the full product surface: patient experience, provider operations, internal tooling, and data infrastructure.

## Head of Product (August 2023 – April 2025)

Took over full product ownership as the platform scaled aggressively. The core challenge was growing revenue without growing the team — which meant finding leverage through automation, better tooling, and smarter systems.

- Re-engineered backend systems and workflows that enabled 4x revenue growth to $20M while maintaining flat staffing
- Automated contractor payroll using Rippling, saving $100K annually and eliminating thousands of hours of manual invoicing within 6 months
- Built AI-assisted patient onboarding tools with Tellescope that reduced manual admin work by 40% and shortened time-to-first-session by 25%
- Implemented analytics instrumentation, attribution modeling, and feedback loops to guide quarterly planning and validate product decisions
- Consolidated fragmented data streams into a unified data warehouse, improving reporting and enabling data-driven decision-making
- Managed a cross-functional team including 2 direct reports across product and operations
- Partnered closely with engineering, clinical, and operations leads to align priorities and resolve platform bottlenecks
- Led initiatives to identify and resolve tech debt that had accumulated during early growth

## Marketing & Founding Product Lead (December 2020 – August 2023)

Built the product function from scratch. When I joined there was no formal product process, no analytics framework, and no clear roadmap. Fixed all of that while also driving multi-state expansion.

- Established the product function from the ground up within 12 months
- Drove multi-state expansion by aligning the tech stack with business goals
- Built a comprehensive analytics framework providing actionable insights for strategic decision-making
- Introduced attribution modeling, funnel tracking, and lead scoring to improve conversion and enable reliable forecasting
- Modernized provider workflows and reduced tech debt, laying the technical foundation for long-term scalability
- Led a team of 3 internal marketing staff and managed external agency relationships across paid, SEO, and creative

## Tech Stack

Tellescope, Rippling, Amplitude, Zapier, Notion, JIRA, various EHR integrations
