# Podcasts I Listen To

## Product and Tech

**Lenny's Podcast** — Probably the best product-focused podcast running right now. Long-form conversations with PMs, founders, and operators at companies worth paying attention to.

**The Knowledge Project** by Shane Parrish — Mental models, decision-making, and conversations with people who think carefully about how to think. Not product-specific but directly applicable.

**Lex Fridman Podcast** — Long, technical, and occasionally self-indulgent but the conversations with AI researchers and engineers are worth it for staying current on where the field is actually going.

**How I Built This** by Guy Raz — Founder stories. Good for understanding the early chaos behind companies that look inevitable in retrospect.

## Business and Strategy

**Acquired** — Deep dives into company histories and business models. The episodes on companies like Costco, LVMH, and Berkshire Hathaway are genuinely excellent.

**Invest Like the Best** by Patrick O'Shaughnessy — Conversations with investors and operators about businesses, strategy, and markets. More applicable to product strategy than the name suggests.

## AI and Building

**No Priors** — AI-focused conversations with researchers and builders. Good for staying current on what's actually happening in the field versus the hype cycle.

**Practical AI** — More applied than theoretical. Useful for understanding what's actually being built with AI in production.
