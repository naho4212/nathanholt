# What I'm Looking For

## Role Types

Targeting Senior PM, Product Lead, and Head of Product roles. The title matters less than the scope — I want end-to-end ownership of a meaningful product surface, close proximity to engineering, and visibility into the business.

Role targeting by company stage:
- Seed / Pre-Series A: Head of Product or Product Lead
- Series A: Head of Product or Senior PM
- Series B: Senior PM
- Series C+: PM or Senior PM

## Company Types

AI-first startups, SaaS platforms, marketplaces, and data-intensive companies. I'm drawn to companies where the product is technically complex or operationally interesting — places where understanding how systems work gives you a real edge.

I'm particularly interested in companies building in: applied AI and automation, healthtech, B2B SaaS, and platforms with complex data or workflow problems.

## What Excites Me

- 0-to-1 product work — building something from scratch and figuring out what it actually needs to be
- Internal tooling and workflow automation — the unsexy work that has outsized impact
- LLM integration into real products and workflows — not wrapping AI, actually wiring it in
- Operationally complex problems where the product and the business are deeply intertwined

## Compensation

Base salary target: $160K–$200K+. Open to equity-heavy packages at early-stage companies.

## Location

Remote preferred. NYC hybrid open. Not open to full-time in-office.

## What I'm Not Looking For

- Pure execution roles with no strategic input
- Companies where product is downstream of sales or engineering with no real ownership
- Roles that are primarily roadmap management without room to shape direction
