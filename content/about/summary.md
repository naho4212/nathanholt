# Summary

## Who I Am

I'm a Senior PM and Product Lead based in New York with 8+ years building platforms, internal tools, and AI-driven systems across healthtech, SaaS, and marketplaces. I've spent most of my career at the intersection of product strategy and technical execution — close enough to the code to understand what's actually hard, but focused enough on the business to know what actually matters.

## The Arc

Started in ecommerce and digital product at Barneys New York and Thorsun, where I learned how to ship things that worked in operationally complex environments. Moved into healthtech with Thriving Center of Psychology, where I joined as the first product hire and eventually became Head of Product, driving the company from early stage to $20M ARR. Most recently I've been advising Dosable on their 0-to-1 platform build while building PowSignal (freshie.ai) as a side project and conducting a targeted job search.

## What I'm Good At

I'm best in situations that require both strategic clarity and hands-on execution. I don't separate those things — the interesting product work lives in the details. My strongest areas are platform architecture, internal tooling, workflow automation, and applied AI. I've shipped LLM integrations, built data warehouses, automated payroll systems, and rebuilt ecommerce stacks from scratch.

I also have genuine technical fluency. I can read and write code (Python, TypeScript, SQL, React), work directly with engineers on architecture decisions, and build working prototypes independently using AI-native tools like Claude Code and Cursor.

## How I Work

I think in systems. I care about how data flows, how edge cases behave in production, and how the thing you ship today creates or forecloses options tomorrow. I'm direct, low-ego, and I'd rather be in a room making a decision than writing a doc about making a decision.

I've managed teams and I've worked solo. I'm comfortable in both — what I care about is that the work is good and the team trusts each other.
